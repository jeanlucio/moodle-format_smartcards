# 🎓 Educational Purpose

SmartCards is designed to:

* Replace the plain, text-heavy default activity list with a visual, icon-first grid — lowering
  the "wall of text" feel of a long course for younger or less experienced students
* Make availability and progress visible at a glance, through badges and progress indicators,
  instead of requiring a click into every activity to find out whether it is accessible
* Let a teacher match the navigation style to the course's own shape — a short course can stay a
  flat grid, a long one can collapse into an accordion, split into tabs, or unfold as a
  step-by-step trail
* Encourage exploration through a more game-like, less bureaucratic presentation, without ever
  hiding an activity's real availability or restriction reason from the student

Suitable for:

* Any course that benefits from a more visual, less list-like presentation
* Courses with many sections or activities, where collapsible or step-based navigation reduces
  scrolling and cognitive load
* Onboarding or orientation courses, where a clear visual sense of what is available and what is
  already done matters as much as the content itself
